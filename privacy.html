Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us — here's exactly how we handle your data

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Applies to jasper0283.github.io (BrickPick)

1. Who We Are

BrickPick ("we", "us", "our") is a recommendation website operated at jasper0283.github.io. We help visitors find the best LEGO sets through curated reviews and comparisons. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by the LEGO Group.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: contact@brickpick.com

2. Information We Collect

BrickPick is a static website and does not collect personal information directly. We do not have login forms, comment sections, or checkout processes. However, third-party tools we use may collect data as described below:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website or search query
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies through third-party services including:

  • Google Analytics — to understand how visitors use our site (anonymous, aggregated data)
  • Amazon Associates — affiliate tracking cookies when you click our product links

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to browse BrickPick, but may affect affiliate tracking.

4. How We Use Information

Any data collected through analytics is used solely to:

  • Understand which content is most helpful to visitors
  • Improve site performance and user experience
  • Identify popular product categories

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to third-party websites such as Amazon.com and LEGO.com. When you click these links, you leave our site and are subject to the privacy policies of those websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before making a purchase.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of any third-party sites.

6. Children's Privacy

BrickPick does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our site is intended for general audiences, including parents researching products for their children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our site, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights & Contact

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), or other privacy laws, including the right to access, correct, or delete data held about you.

To exercise any of these rights or ask questions about this policy, contact us at:

Email: contact@brickpick.com

We will respond within 30 days.
